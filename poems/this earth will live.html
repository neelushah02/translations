this earth will live

I have faith
This earth will live
If nowhere else then in my very bones
She will live like termites live
In the bark of trees
Like weevils live hidden in grain
Even after the apocalypse she will live within me
If nowhere else then on my tongue
And in my impermanence
She will live

And one morning I will wake
I will wake with the earth
With the water and turtles I will wake
I will wake and set off to meet
The person I have promised
I will.

जैसे दाने में रह लेता है घुन
यह रहेगी प्रलय के बाद भी मेरे अन्दर
यदि और कहीं नहीं तो मेरी ज़बान
और मेरी नश्वरता में
